SIGN!

The Revolutionary Sign Language App

Worldly & user-friendly!

Experience and make most out of this communication platform for every regardless of physical and personal barriers.

As an app with a social advocacy, SIGN seeks to make an accessible channel for the deaf and mute and their friends and people they want to interact with to connect.

An ideal app to build positive relations

SIGN will bridge the gaps between the deaf and the mute & the people they seek to interact with. By becoming a translator for users to understand sign language, they can efficiently communicate with the PWDs. This allows them to finally create a productive discourse and develop an unstoppable friendship.

An app that promotes inclusivity

Now that we are in the middle of the 21st century, the world is now considered to be a home for everyone regardless of their status. Therefore, people with disabilities and special needs are also prioritized and justified. SIGN reaches out to the deaf and mute as a special app for their social well-being.

An app for Disability Awareness & Appreciation

Because the people with disabilities are humans with dignity and the right to be heard, they are given a chance to be treated with kindness in this society. Through SIGN, they would feel welcomed and a sense of belongingness because an effort is made to understand them and finally talk to them. As an innovative platform, it is definitely special for people with disabilities like the deaf & the mute.
