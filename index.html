About Us

We're three entrepreneurs who believe that lack of knowledge should not be a barrier between the deaf/mute community and the rest of the world. We're just three passionate girls who believe that nothing should get in the way of us communicating with other people. There are multiple apps that help us translate languages all over the world, there are even real time translators that allows you to speak into a device, and voila! You can speak with anyone around the world. Unfortunately, sign language doesn't get the same attention. This is a gap, a barrier, a problem that we want to solve.

Our Application

Friendly User Interface

SIGN offers an easy-to-use and easy-to-learn user interface that is also disability-friendly! SIGN is easy, simple, and quick. Just press the camera button and you'll get the chance to communicate with anyone.

Availability

SIGN will soon be able in over 150 countries all over the world as it helps build bridges from the deaf/mute community and the rest of the world.

Constant Development

HearTheWorld Co. is constantly improving SIGN. Every minute, newer and better gadgets are being released and SIGN won't be left behind. We are constantly developing SIGN to provide all our users with an app that is worthy of their time.

Connections

SIGN can translate from over 50+ sign languages to more than 100+ spoken languages including but not limited to: Chinese, Japanese, Filipino, and English.

Frequently Asked Questions

Will the app be available to IOS and Android devices?

Our app is availabe for both operating platforms.

Will the app be free?

Our app's main features will be free, but to access the Sign Language lessons available, a monthly subscription fee has to be paid.

Can it translate from spoken/written language to sign language?

Yes! SIGN! will support two-way translation between 50+ sign languages and over 150+ spoken and written languages.

Can we use SIGN! without wi-fi?

The main translator can be used offline and online, but some functions will be limited without wif-fi.
